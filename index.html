Calculation Methodology

Energy Savings Calculation

Annual energy savings are calculated based on the difference between baseline and efficient appliance energy consumption:

Energy Savings (kWh/year) = (Baseline Power - Efficient Power) × Daily Operating Hours × 365

For air conditioners: Power = Cooling Capacity (kW) ÷ CSPF

Emissions Reduction

CO₂ emissions reduction is calculated using country-specific grid emission factors:

CO₂ Reduction (tCO₂/year) = Energy Savings (kWh) × Grid Emission Factor (kgCO₂/kWh) ÷ 1000

Default GEF: 0.5 kgCO₂/kWh (Sub-Saharan Africa average). Country-specific factors available in database.

Cost Savings

Financial savings based on local electricity tariffs:

Annual Savings (USD) = Energy Savings (kWh) × Electricity Tariff (USD/kWh)

Default tariff: $0.15/kWh. Country-specific tariffs stored in database.

Performance Metric Standards

Air Conditioners - CSPF

Cooling Seasonal Performance Factor measures average efficiency across seasonal conditions.

Baseline (Fixed Speed)CSPF 3.0
U4E Minimum (Level 3)CSPF 3.5
Advanced (Inverter)CSPF ≥ 4.5
Best PracticeCSPF ≥ 5.5

Refrigerators - EEI

Energy Efficiency Index compares consumption to reference model (lower is better).

InefficientEEI > 150
ECOWAS RegionalEEI ≤ 140
SADC HarmonizedEEI ≤ 120
Global Best PracticeEEI ≤ 100

Electric Motors - IE Class

IEC 60034-30-1 International Efficiency classification.

IE1 Standard~80% efficient
IE2 High Efficiency~86% efficient
IE3 Premium~91% efficient
IE4 Super Premium~93% efficient

Lighting - Efficacy (lm/W)

Lumens per watt measures light output efficiency.

Incandescent~12 lm/W
CFL~60 lm/W
LED Basic (MEPS)≥80 lm/W
LED Premium≥120 lm/W

Gap-Filling Methodology

When country-specific data is unavailable, the following approaches are applied:

Grid Emission Factors

  • Primary: IEA Emissions Factors 2024 (country-specific where available)
  • Secondary: IPCC Tier 1 default factors by fuel mix
  • Fallback: Regional average (Sub-Saharan Africa: 0.50 kgCO₂/kWh)

Electricity Tariffs

  • Primary: National utility tariffs (Dec 2024)
  • Secondary: GlobalPetrolPrices.com database
  • Fallback: Regional average (Africa: $0.134/kWh residential)

Appliance Stock

  • Primary: U4E/CLASP market assessments (country-specific)
  • Secondary: IEA Africa Outlook extrapolations
  • Fallback: Population × ownership rate (AC: 5-15%, Refrigerator: 20-40%)

Appliance Prices

  • Primary: CLASP market studies, U4E assessments
  • Secondary: Online retailer surveys (Jumia, etc.)
  • Fallback: Regional average (AC: $380-450 USD, Refrigerator: $300-400 USD)
Uncertainty: Gap-filled values are marked in the database. Typical uncertainty ranges: Grid factors ±10%, Tariffs ±5%, Stock ±30%, Prices ±20%.

Data Quality Assurance

  • Country data verified against official standards bodies
  • Regional status confirmed with SADC, ECOWAS, EAC
  • CSPF/EEI/IE cross-referenced with U4E Model Regs
  • Emission factors aligned with IPCC guidelines
  • Tariffs updated quarterly from national utilities
  • Database last updated: December 2024
